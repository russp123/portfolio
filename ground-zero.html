Service Offer Page

Post-Masterclass Sales Page

Ground Zero is the page a warm lead lands on right after the masterclass and scorecard — "You're closer than you think." Its whole job is to turn interest into a paid next step. I designed and built it as a two-path offer: a full Ground Zero + Consultation package with a tiered pricing table, or a lower-commitment Viability Consultation, each framed so the visitor self-selects and moves forward. Below the offer it explains the design service in 3D, walks through exactly what happens in the consultation, and closes out the objections in an FAQ.

This is a showcase of the page itself — the layout, the offer architecture, and the conversion flow, captured as built.
